## Key Challenges & Risks

### 1. Network Effects Hurdle

**Problem:** App is only valuable if whole group uses it
**Mitigation:**
- Make onboarding frictionless (1-click invites)
- Offer value even for partial adoption (one person can track for group)
- Create incentives for inviting members (gamification, rewards)

### 2. Mobile-First World

**Problem:** Web-only limits adoption (people check on phones)
**Mitigation:**
- Build mobile PWA immediately (installable, offline-capable)
- Prioritize native mobile app (React Native for iOS/Android)
- Design mobile-first, then scale up for desktop

### 3. Seasonal Usage

**Problem:** Ski season is 5-6 months, app goes dormant in summer
**Mitigation:**
- Expand to multi-season (summer lake houses, beach houses)
- Add year-round features (trip planning for next season)
- Off-season engagement (gear sales, early bird weather)

### 4. Competitive Moat

**Problem:** Idea is easily replicable by larger players
**Mitigation:**
- Move fast, build loyal user base
- Focus on ski-specific depth (not breadth)
- Build community and brand (Mokki = ski house coordination)
- Establish resort partnerships early (exclusive data)

### 5. Customer Acquisition Cost

**Problem:** Expensive to reach niche ski house organizers
**Mitigation:**
- Leverage organic channels (Reddit, forums, word-of-mouth)
- Referral programs with strong incentives
- Partner with existing communities (ski clubs, forums)
- Content marketing to drive inbound
